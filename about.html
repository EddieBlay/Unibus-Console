About us

Read more about Unibus

Unibus station is a government sponsored project which was built to provide unified bus services across the whole of the Great Britain. It was built in the year 2021.

The station offers three types of bus services, namely, Local bus services, Regional bus services and National bus services.

You can find the following facilities to make your time as comfortable as possible:


Waiting facilities

Restaurants

Coffee shops

Shopping malls

Hotels

Toilets facilities

Recreational centres, etc.

As the biggest bus station across the United Kingdom, we are always ready to serve you.
